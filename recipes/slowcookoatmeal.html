Slow Cook Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal so creamy it's like tapioca! You will absolutely love this recipe. This is how oatmeal was meant to be!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup half-and-half cream
  • Â¼ cup brown sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Just before going to bed, combine the oats and water in a slow cooker.
  2. Set the slow cooker on Low, cover, and let cook overnight.
  3. In the morning, stir in the salt and half-and-half.
  4. Scoop the oatmeal into bowls.
  5. Sprinkle brown sugar over the top.
